About ShareHive

🐝 ShareHive is your all-in-one personal management system — notes, bookmarks, videos, to-do lists, expenses, planning, wiki, uploads, projects, courses, and more — everything organized in one place.

Our Mission

To simplify productivity and knowledge management by offering an integrated, user-friendly tool accessible right from your browser.

Why ShareHive?

  • ✅ Keep all your personal data in one place.
  • ✅ Privacy-first, stored in your browser using IndexedDB.
  • ✅ Easy, clean, and intuitive design.
  • ✅ Expandable for more features in the future.
