📜 Privacy & Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Below you’ll find details about how ShareHive handles your data.

🔒 Data Storage

All your data (notes, bookmarks, todos, etc.) is stored locally on your browser using IndexedDB. We do not send your data to external servers.

🛡️ Security

Since your data is only stored locally, you control it completely. You can also export/import your data anytime via the Settings page.

📧 Communication

We may use your email only when you contact us via our contact form. We do not share your email with third parties.

⚖️ Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be updated as new features are added. We encourage you to check this page regularly.

❓ Questions

If you have any questions, feel free to contact us.
